# The Bitcoin Diploma Story

There is nothing more powerful than an idea whose time has come.  
This story begins in El Salvador in 2022, when we launched the world’s first Bitcoin Diploma inside a public school system. We did not ask the government for permission. The school invited us, and 38 students graduated that June.

Some criticized us for bypassing official channels. When the first students crossed the stage, we announced expansion to a second school the next day.

A year later, the same government asked for help introducing bitcoin education. To protect our independence, we refused payment and instead trained hundreds of public-school teachers to run the program themselves.

In September 2022 we made the entire curriculum open source. What began as a local experiment quickly became a global movement.

Today the curriculum is updated twice a year and adapted by educators worldwide.  
It now includes a one-hour introduction and a program for juniors.

In March 2023 we launched the Education Network, a global community built on shared principles: independent, impartial, community-led, bitcoin-only, high-quality education. Since then, educators have translated the curriculum and brought it to 41 countries across six continents.

Because the work is open source, many others teach it independently without our knowledge or permission.

The Bitcoin Diploma succeeds because it reflects what it teaches: empowerment through permissionless innovation. It grows through collaboration, not competition. Through impact, not profit.

Independent, community-led bitcoin education will change the world. It already has.

For a better world,  
My First Bitcoin team, 2026
