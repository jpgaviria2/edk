# Bitcoin Diploma

The **Bitcoin Diploma** is My First Bitcoin’s flagship educational program.

It provides a **comprehensive and structured introduction to Bitcoin**, designed to give learners a deep understanding of:

- what Bitcoin is,
- how it works,
- why it matters,
- and how to use it responsibly.

This program is used by educators, students, and partner organizations worldwide.

## 🎯 Educational objectives

By completing the Bitcoin Diploma, learners should be able to:

- explain the core concepts and principles of Bitcoin,
- understand the technical foundations at a high level,
- safely use Bitcoin for self-custody and transactions,
- critically evaluate common misconceptions and narratives around Bitcoin.

The focus is on **understanding, sovereignty, and long-term thinking**, not on speculation or trading.

## 👥 Target audience

The Bitcoin Diploma is designed for:

- beginners with no prior Bitcoin knowledge,
- students and community learners,
- educators and trainers,
- anyone seeking a solid conceptual foundation in Bitcoin.

No technical or financial background is required.
