## Concepts to teach

- Definition of money
- Direct barter and double coincidence of wants
- Indirect barter and emergence of the most saleable good
- Functions of money: medium of exchange, store of value, unit of account
- Properties of money: durability, portability, fungibility, acceptability, scarcity, divisibility
- Commodity money, coinage, paper receipts, fiat money
- Debasement, inflation, purchasing power, and trust
- Bitcoin as sound digital money
