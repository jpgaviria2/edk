What Is Money?

Before Bitcoin makes sense, money has to make sense. Learn the three jobs money performs and what makes some money better than others.

StartHost this lesson
Learner progress

One block. One core idea. One next action.

Lesson path

Short, guided, and hard to get lost in.

1

Experience the problem

Imagine trading without prices or a common payment good. Every exchange depends on finding the perfect match.

2

Name the concept

Money helps as a medium of exchange, unit of account, and store of value.

3

Compare candidates

Score bananas, dollars, gold, and Bitcoin against durability, portability, divisibility, scarcity, and acceptance.

Try it

Which function is this?

A café lists espresso at $3 and a sandwich at $9. What job is money doing?

Reflect

Make it yours

Write one sentence explaining why society needs money.

Host mode version

Run this with a group.

Use candy, a real dollar, and a play-money bill. Let the group decide which one they would accept, then ask why.
