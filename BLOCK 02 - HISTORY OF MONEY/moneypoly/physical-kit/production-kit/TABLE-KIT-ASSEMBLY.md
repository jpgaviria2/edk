# Table Kit Assembly

Prepare **8 identical table kits**.

## Each table kit contains
- 1 table sign
- 5 role packets
- 5 have/need cards
- 1 commodity acceptance sign
- 1 mini price board
- 1 local event deck
- 10 final scorecards
- 1 score sheet for banker
- 2 ledger sheets
- 12 credit slips
- 10 wallet cards set
- 10 fake seed phrase cards
- 1 pen bundle
- 1 token tray set

## Packet assembly order

### Role packet for each team
1. role card
2. starting goods
3. have/need card
4. next-generation goal marker
5. one short rules strip

## Color coding
Use color by era:
- green = barter/resources
- cream = commodity money
- gold = gold phase
- purple = Medici/ledger
- blue = fiat
- orange = Bitcoin

## Final check before bagging
- correct table number inserted
- all 5 roles present
- no real seed phrases anywhere
- enough starting resources for all teams
- banker sheet clipped to top
