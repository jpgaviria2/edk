Facilitator Cheat Sheet

Goal: survive today, preserve wealth, pass value to the next generation.

Order: barter → commodity → gold → ledgers → fiat → bitcoin.

Barter: feel double coincidence of wants.

Commodity: acceptance lowers friction.

Gold: harder money, harder portability.

Ledgers: trust and signatures matter.

Fiat: convenience with debasement/control risk.

Bitcoin: scarce digital money with self-custody tradeoffs.

Close: the deepest question is what preserves the fruits of work across time.
