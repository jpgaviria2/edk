# Scoring Rubric

## Categories
- Food secured: 1
- Water secured: 1
- Shelter secured: 1
- Next-generation need secured: 2
- Wealth preserved after crisis: 3
- No seizure/freeze loss: 2
- Used strong money properties effectively: 3
- Can explain why the system worked or failed: 3

## Facilitator note
The point is not perfect arithmetic. The point is comparative learning signal across eras.
