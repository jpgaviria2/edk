# Staffing Plan

## Minimum staff for 80 participants

### 1. Lead facilitator (1)
Owns:
- room energy
- teaching arc
- transitions between eras
- final debrief

### 2. AV / timekeeper (1)
Owns:
- projector
- countdown timer
- slide/app projection
- round-change cues

### 3. Table bankers (4)
Each banker covers **2 tables**.

Owns:
- distributing era materials
- resolving disputes
- issuing slips during Medici phase
- applying event effects
- collecting quick scoring signals

### 4. Roaming event master (1)
Owns:
- event deck
- scarcity/inflation/confiscation/bandit injections
- replacing missing materials
- helping overloaded bankers

## Preferred extra role

### Setup runner / floater (1)
Owns:
- late arrivals
- refilling tokens
- fixing packet mixups
- table reset support

## Pre-brief checklist for staff

Every staff member should know:
- the 7 phases
- the one-sentence goal of each phase
- the difference between survival and generational wealth
- that the room is not aiming for perfect simulation
- that pace matters more than precision

## Banker quick script

### Barter
"Direct trade only. Both sides must actually want what the other offers."

### Commodity money
"Now shells are accepted by many traders, even if they do not want your exact good."

### Gold
"Gold stores value better, but it is heavier, riskier, and harder for change."

### Credit & ledgers
"Deposit gold, receive signed slips, and trust the branch ledger. Unsigned slips are invalid."

### Fiat
"These notes are easy to use, but supply and policy can change. Watch prices."

### Bitcoin
"Sats are scarce and divisible. Choose convenience or stronger self-custody."
