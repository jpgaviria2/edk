# Reset and Strike Checklist

## Immediate post-session
- collect scorecards
- collect wallet, ledger, and slip cards
- count reusable tokens back into bins
- separate damaged vs reusable items

## Table reset
- rebuild 5 role packets per table
- restack event deck
- restock price board and acceptance signs
- verify banker clipboard is complete

## Final strike
- remove signage and tape
- pack AV gear
- pack remaining supply bins by category
- log shortages for next run
