# Core Game Loop

## The core rule
The **board stays the same**.
The **goal stays the same**.
The only thing that changes from era to era is the **method of trading / money layer**.

## Constant player goal
Each team is always trying to secure:
- food essentials
- water essentials
- shelter essentials
- one additional savings asset for the future

### Recommended scoring target
A team should try to end each era with at least:
- **5 food units**
- **5 water units**
- **5 shelter points or equivalent shelter access**
- **1 savings asset** set aside

Optional harder mode:
- **10 food**
- **10 water**
- **10 shelter value**
- **2 savings assets**

## What changes by era
### Era 1 — Barter
The board spaces are the same, but trades must be completed directly in goods.

Example:
- mangoes for water
- fish for shelter
- cows for food

### Era 2 — Commodity Money
The same board spaces exist, but now shells can be used as an additional trading medium.

Example:
- mangoes for water
- **or** shells for water

### Era 3 — Gold
The same board spaces exist, but gold can now be used to settle trades.

Example:
- mangoes for water
- shells for water
- **or** gold for water

### Era 4 — Credit & Ledgers
The same board spaces exist, but signed slips and ledger balances can settle some trades.

### Era 5 — Fiat
The same board spaces exist, but paper notes / dollars can settle trades.

### Era 6 — Bitcoin
The same board spaces exist, but sats can settle trades.

## Design principle
Do **not** redesign the board each era.

Do **not** change the underlying human need.

The educational point is:
> same people, same needs, same world, same board — different money.

## What the player should feel
- barter = friction
- commodity money = better saleability
- gold = stronger savings, harder portability
- ledgers = trust and institutional dependence
- fiat = convenience plus inflation/control risk
- bitcoin = scarce digital money with custody tradeoffs

## Moderator line
"Nothing about your human needs changed. Nothing about the board changed. What changed is how you are able to trade and save."
