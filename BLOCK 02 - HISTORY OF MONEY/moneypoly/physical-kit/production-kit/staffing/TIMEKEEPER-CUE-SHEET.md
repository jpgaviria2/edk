# Timekeeper Cue Sheet

## Signals
- 1 minute left: single chime
- freeze and listen: double chime
- transition now: triple chime

## Standard pace
- intro 5 min
- barter 8 min
- changed needs 6 min
- commodity 8 min
- gold 7 min
- ledger 8 min
- fiat 7 min
- bitcoin 7 min
- debrief 4 min

## Rule
If the room is behind, compress discussion before you cut gameplay.
