# Event Master Cue Sheet

## Cue points
- after barter settles
- after changed-needs round
- midway through commodity phase
- during gold phase
- during fiat phase
- during Bitcoin phase

## Preferred event flow
1. scarcity shock
2. commodity inflation or acceptance mismatch
3. gold robbery or clipping
4. bank run or wrong-city slip failure
5. fiat printing or freeze
6. bitcoin custody event

## Rule
Do not hit every table with different chaos at once. Keep event load readable.
