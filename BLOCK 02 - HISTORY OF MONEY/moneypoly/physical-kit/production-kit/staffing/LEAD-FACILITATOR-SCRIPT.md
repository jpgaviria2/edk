# Lead Facilitator Script

## Opening
"Tonight you’re going to experience the history of money, not hear a lecture about it. Your challenge is simple: survive today, preserve wealth through chaos, and leave something useful to the next generation."

## Barter prompt
"Trade directly. If both sides don’t want what the other offers, the trade fails."

## Commodity prompt
"Now a commonly accepted good can bridge wants that don’t line up. Watch what happens to trade friction."

## Gold prompt
"Gold improves savings, but introduces weight, verification, and seizure risk."

## Ledger prompt
"Paper claims become useful only because people trust branches, signatures, and ledgers."

## Fiat prompt
"Easy money can still be weak money if supply and control change."

## Bitcoin prompt
"Crises still happen. The question is which crises damage goods and markets, and which crises damage the money itself."

## Close
"Money matters because time matters. The hardest money is the money that best preserves the fruits of your work across time."
