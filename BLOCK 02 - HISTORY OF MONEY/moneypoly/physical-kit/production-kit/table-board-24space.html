Objective

Win condition

  • Secure food
  • Secure water
  • Secure shelter
  • Set aside at least 1 savings asset
Turn loop

How each round works

  1. Moderator rolls for everyone
  2. All teams move the same number of spaces
  3. Each table resolves landed spaces locally
  4. Teams passing GO collect production
  5. Moderator reveals next roll or event
GO payouts

Fixed production

  • Water +5
  • Fish +4
  • Cow +1
  • Shelter +1
  • Coffee +6
  • Shell Collector +4 shells
  • Merchant = choose 1 small good
Space rule

Constant board, changing money

The Water Trade space is always the Water Trade space. What changes is how you pay there:

Barter: goods only Commodity: goods or shells Gold: goods, shells, or gold Ledgers: goods, gold, or valid slips Fiat: goods or paper notes Bitcoin: goods or sats
