# Monopoly-Style Board Layout (24 Spaces)

This is the recommended live board for **moderator mode**.

## Design target
- **24 spaces** around the perimeter
- works for **up to 8 players or 8 teams per table**
- one shared board per table
- all tables use the same layout
- moderator rolls once for the whole room
- the board stays the same across all eras
- only the trade method changes from barter → shells → gold → slips → fiat → sats

## Why 24 spaces
This is the sweet spot:
- feels like a real Monopoly-style loop
- enough variety to introduce systems gradually
- not so big that players wait forever to pass GO
- supports 6 to 8 players per table without feeling cramped

## Player count
### Best
- **6 players/teams per table** = ideal clarity

### Maximum
- **8 players/teams per table** = still workable

If you run 8 per table, use smaller tokens and tighter time discipline.

## Board structure
Use a perimeter board with **4 corners + 5 spaces per side between corners**.

## Corner logic
### GO / Production
Collect role production.

### Community Well
Public resource zone. Can be used for water-focused event or recovery effects.

### Trade Route
Best place for optional cross-table trade windows.

### Vault / Treasury
Strong place for gold, slips, fiat reserve, or protected savings rules.

## Space behavior menu
Every landed space should give a player **2 to 4 clear options max**.

### 1. GO / Production
- collect role payout
- no purchase needed

### 2. Water Trade
- buy water at posted price
- sell water if allowed
- skip

### 3. Open Market A
- choose 1 posted trade from market card
- skip

### 4. Food Trade
- buy food at posted price
- sell food if allowed
- skip

### 5. Crisis Space A
- resolve active stage event if it targets you
- or draw/apply local effect if instructed

### 6. Commodity Exchange
- convert goods into shells / beans / beads in relevant era
- skip

### 7. Community Well
- receive emergency water if event/rule allows
- otherwise no effect

### 8. Shelter Trade
- buy shelter
- sell shelter if allowed
- skip

### 9. Open Market B
- choose 1 posted trade
- skip

### 10. Fish Trade
- buy fish
- sell fish if allowed
- skip

### 11. Gold Market
- convert into gold
- sell gold for goods or money if era allows
- skip

### 12. Government Levy
- pay tax / lose turn / obey policy effect

### 13. Trade Route
- optional cross-table trade window when moderator allows
- otherwise standard market action

### 14. Medici Bank
- deposit gold
- receive signed slip
- redeem valid slip
- skip

### 15. Open Market C
- choose 1 posted trade
- skip

### 16. Cattle Trade
- buy cows
- sell cows
- skip

### 17. Crisis Space B
- resolve active stage event if targeted
- or local rule if instructed

### 18. Central Bank
- receive fiat
- exchange into fiat if rule allows
- resolve money-printing or freeze effects

### 19. Vault / Treasury
- protect gold or savings if current era supports it
- deposit into reserve zone if rules allow

### 20. Bitcoin Savings
- convert into sats
- choose hot or cold custody
- protect savings

### 21. Lightning Payment
- make one instant small sats payment
- clear one low-value trade faster than normal

### 22. Open Market D
- choose 1 posted trade
- skip

### 23. Generational Wealth Check
- set aside savings for final score
- protect one asset class if the rules allow

### 24. Ruler / Policy Space
- resolve confiscation, decree, policy, or subsidy rule

## Recommended posted trades
The game runs better if the trade spaces use **posted menus**, not endless negotiation.

The important rule is: **the space stays the same, but the payment method evolves by era.**

Examples at the exact same Water Trade space:
- barter era: 10 mangoes for 1 water bundle
- commodity era: 10 mangoes **or** 2 shells for 1 water bundle
- gold era: 10 mangoes **or** 2 shells **or** 1 gold fraction for 1 water bundle
- fiat era: 10 mangoes **or** dollars for 1 water bundle
- bitcoin era: sats for 1 water bundle

That means the educational lesson is always visible: same need, same trade location, different money tool.

## Recommended table roles for up to 8 players
Use up to 8 distinct producer identities:
1. Mango Producer
2. Water Producer
3. Fish Producer
4. Cow Producer
5. Shelter Producer
6. Coffee Producer
7. Salt / Shell Collector
8. Merchant / Trader

## GO payouts for 8-player mode
Use fixed payouts:
- Mango Producer → +10 mangoes
- Water Producer → +5 water
- Fish Producer → +4 fish
- Cow Producer → +1 cow
- Shelter Producer → +1 shelter
- Coffee Producer → +6 coffee units
- Salt/Shell Collector → +4 shell units
- Merchant/Trader → choose 1 small good from market reserve

## Space design rule
A player should be able to land, understand the choice, and act in **under 20 seconds**.

If a space invites open-ended bargaining every time, it is too slow for stage mode.

This **24-space perimeter board** should become the default master board for the large meetup version.
