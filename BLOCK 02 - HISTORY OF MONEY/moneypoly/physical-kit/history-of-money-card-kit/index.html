Print notes

  1. Use Download PDF if you want the ready-made file.
  2. Print fronts first on cardstock or heavy matte paper.
  3. Reinsert sheets and print mirrored backs using flip on long edge.
  4. Cut along the light crop marks. Optional: sleeve or round the corners.
