Print notes

  1. The live print buttons now work best in Landscape 8-up poker size layout.
  2. Use Download safest B&W PDF if your printer or phone preview is clipping near the edges.
  3. Use Download standard B&W PDF for the denser 9-up black-and-white layout.
  4. Use Download color PDF if you want the richer version.
  5. Print fronts first on cardstock or heavy matte paper.
  6. Reinsert sheets and print mirrored backs using flip on long edge.
  7. Cut along the light crop marks. Optional: sleeve or round the corners.
