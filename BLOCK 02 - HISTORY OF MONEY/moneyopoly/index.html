    How to play

    Barter Moneyopoly

    1. You play as Maya, the mango farmer.
    2. Roll dice to move around the board and collect goods.
    3. You need food, water, and shelter to survive.
    4. There is no money, so every trade must be direct barter.
    5. Every 3 rounds, an event card changes the market.
    6. If trades fail, that is the lesson: barter needs a double coincidence of wants.
