Moneyopoly: Barter Market

Play a tiny survival market before money exists. Every player has something valuable, but trade only works when wants line up.

Facilitator notes
Lesson objective

Feel the friction first.

Players need food, water, and shelter. They can only barter directly. Every 3 rounds, an event changes the market.

  • No money yet
  • Direct trades only
  • Goods can spoil or get hit by events
  • Win by surviving with useful surplus

What the game is teaching

Before money, trade is fragile.

In barter, having value is not enough. You need someone who wants exactly what you have and has exactly what you need.

    Facilitator mode

    How to run this physically

    1. Give each learner one role card: Mango Farmer, Cattle Herder, Water Keeper, Builder.
    2. Give each player surplus of their own good and shortage in another survival need.
    3. Let them trade for 3 minutes with no money and no central exchange.
    4. Trigger an event card: flood, drought, pests, spoiled food.
    5. Debrief the pain: “Who had value but still could not trade?”
