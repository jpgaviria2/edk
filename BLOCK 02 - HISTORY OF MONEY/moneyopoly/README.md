# Moneyopoly — Barter Market MVP

Prototype 1 for the Block 02 flagship History of Money game.

## Learning goal

Make learners *feel* the limits of barter before introducing commodity money, fiat money, and Bitcoin.

## Current game

- 4 player roles: Mango Farmer, Cattle Herder, Water Keeper, Builder
- 4 resources: mangoes, cows, water, shelter
- 12 rounds
- Every 3 rounds, an event changes the market
- Direct barter only
- Failed trades are counted as the main learning signal
- End-game debrief introduces double coincidence of wants and the reason money emerges

## Next eras

1. Commodity Moneyopoly — one widely accepted good lowers trade friction
2. Shell Moneyopoly — easy money supply shock creates inflation
3. Coin Moneyopoly — durable money plus debasement risk
4. Paper Receipt Moneyopoly — portability plus custodian trust risk
5. Fiat Moneyopoly — money printing and purchasing-power loss
6. Digital Bank Moneyopoly — speed plus censorship/custody risk
7. Bitcoin Moneyopoly — fixed supply, self-custody, verification, Lightning
