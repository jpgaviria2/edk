Teaching standard

Every block becomes a complete learning kit.

  • Learn — concise curriculum-backed explanation
  • Play — practical game or simulator
  • Teach — facilitator flow and prompts
  • Print — classroom materials and physical activity
  • Apply — real-world scenario or decision
